Game Name: God of War (2018)
Release Date: 04/20/2018
Studio: Santa Monica Studio
Genre: Action-Adventure
Synopsis: 
God of War (2018) follows Kratos and his son Atreus on a journey to scatter the
ashes of Kratos' late wife, Faye, from the highest peak in the realms. Along the
way, they face gods and mosters, including the relentless Baldur. Kratos
struggles to guide Atreus while hiding his divine past. Eventually, they learn
Atreus is actually Loki, and Faye eas a giant. After defeating Baldur, Freya vows
revenge, and a prophecy hints at Kratos' fate and the coming of Ragnarok.
