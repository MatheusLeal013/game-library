Game Name: Grand Theft Auto V (GTA V)
Release Date: 09/17/2013
Studio: Rockstar Games
Genre: Open world
Synopsis: 
Grand Theft Auto V follows three criminals—Michael, a retired bank robber; Franklin, a young hustler
looking for bigger opportunities; and Trevor, a violent and unpredictable outlaw—as they team up for
a series of high-stakes heists in the city of Los Santos. As they navigate a world of corruption, crime,
and betrayal, they must outmaneuver law enforcement, rival gangs, and their own personal conflicts.
The story explores themes of ambition, loyalty, and the consequences of a life of crime, with multiple
endings based on the player's choices.
